Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Overview

PEARS is a non-custodial interface that sources quotes from third-party aggregators and lets users submit transactions from their own wallets on the Base network. We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing a transparent and secure trading experience.

Key Point: PEARS does not custody funds, does not execute trades on behalf of users, and does not require account creation or personal information to use our interface.

Data We Process

  • Web Server Logs: IP addresses, user-agent strings, timestamps, and requested URLs for security and performance monitoring
  • On-Chain Data: Public wallet addresses when you connect your wallet (this data is already public on the blockchain)
  • Error Telemetry: Technical error information to improve service reliability
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized data about page views and user interactions to improve our interface

What We Do NOT Process

  • Seed phrases or private keys
  • Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Custodial balances or transaction details beyond public blockchain data
  • Trading history or portfolio information
  • KYC/AML documentation

Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal, privacy-focused analytics to understand how users interact with our interface. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. Essential cookies may be used for:

  • Remembering your wallet connection preferences
  • Maintaining session state during your visit
  • Preventing spam and abuse

Data Retention

  • Server Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Error Reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for up to 1 year to identify long-term usage patterns

Third-Party Services

PEARS integrates with third-party services to provide functionality:

  • 0x Protocol: For DEX aggregation and routing (subject to 0x's privacy policy)
  • Wallet Providers: MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc. (subject to their respective privacy policies)
  • RPC Providers: For blockchain data access (subject to their privacy policies)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the limited data we process. However, since we are a non-custodial platform, the security of your funds ultimately depends on your wallet security practices.

Your Rights

Given the minimal data we collect and our non-custodial nature, there is limited personal data to manage. However, you can:

  • Clear your browser data to remove any local preferences
  • Use browser privacy settings to limit data collection
  • Contact us if you have specific privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
